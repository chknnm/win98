Superorganism - Something For Your M.I.N.D.

Superorganism fan made by RDR

    Something For Your M.I.N.D. feels like someone is working on something they have to get done yet your mind wants to do anything but, constantly wandering in thought about past events and the opinions of others about your own actions, focusing on random things within the room because it's not the task at hand, simply spacing out when you close your eyes for a moment and losing track of how long you stared blanked out for. That desire for mental stimulation because you're that bored.
      Here you have the youtube link and credits
      • https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=b0DJ1EXTmvU

 

 

 

 

                           

 
Feel Good

Feel Good by RDR

    FEEL GOOD just feels like a good time with a brief moment of writer's block where the main character couldn't create something they wanted to but then found some joy in what they had created before moving forward with their day and seemingly embracing the more adult responsibility side of life without issue even using it to renew that creative power and produce something they love.

Soda City Funk by RDR

    Soda City Funk feels like watching someone looking back on their childhood and realizing how much they've drifted apart from everyone else as they've grown. Like they no longer feel like they belong and long to go back to when they felt like they were part of the group, like they deserve to be around their friends.

City Love Red

City Love Red - By RDR

    Citylove feels like several friends who are still left in a group of friends dealing with the crushing reality of growing up. Those long nights together spent playing games, watching TV, and losing themselves in their hobbies are getting harder and harder. Fewer and fewer of the group can make it each time as life's responsibilities keep everyone too busy to make time like when they were younger. No one wants to say it but everyone feels it.
